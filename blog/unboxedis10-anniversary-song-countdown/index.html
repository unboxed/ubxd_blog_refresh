Unboxedis10 anniversary song countdown

As we celebrate our 10th birthday next week, we thought we’d look back at what song was riding high as UK #1 on 29th April 2005. Much to our surprise, it was Peter Kay singing ‘Is This The Way To Amarillo?’ along with Tony Christie, wearing a purple suit and a jazzy shirt to go with it (are you sure this isn’t you in the video, Richard?):

I thought it would make for some good nostalgia to look back at what the UK #1 song was on the birthday of 10 Unboxed people, then get a few words from them on their “anniversary” song.

So we start the countdown…

##10. Sarah:

Sonia - You’ll Never Stop Me Loving You

Sarah’s comment:_“I hadn’t heard this until I had to search for it! I wish it has been something better, 1989 had some crackers.”_ ##9. [Claire](https://www.unboxedconsulting.com/people/claire-kemp): ## Bee Gees - Stayin’ Alive

Claire’s comment: “This is the beat for CPR repetition. I had a 70’s party for my 21st, we played it. I also still like to listen to it but not too often!“

8. Shirin:

Mariah Carey - Without You

Shirin’s comment: “On a serious note, you can’t live without food.”

7. Chris:

Bombalurina - Itsy Bitsy Teeny Weeny Yellow Polka Dot Bikini

Chris’ comment: “God, this is embarrassing.”

6. Tyler:

Bryan Adams - (Everything I Do) I Do It for You

Tyler’s comment: ”‘Don’t tell me it’s not worth fighting for.’ Wise words, Bryan Adams. This song has two guitar solos in it. How rad is that? (Shortcuts - 2:43 onwards & 4:50)“.

5. Tom S:

Wet Wet Wet - Goodnight Girl

Tom’s comment: "I have no recollection of this, nice haircut though.”

4. Neil:

Foreigner - I Want To Know What Love Is

Neil’s comment: "I definitely know the song, can’t say I have any particular attachment to it”

3. Graeme:

Marc Almond & Gene Pitney - Something’s Gotten Hold Of My Heart

Graeme’s comment: “This song gives me the creeps… Given the choice, I’d definitely go for the US #1 on my birthday, Paula Abdul - Straight Up. (Would probably choose Paula Abdul in most situation).”

2. Carrie:

The Boomtown Rats - I Don’t Like Mondays

): Carrie’s comment: _“It’s not true that I don’t like Mondays. But I **do** like rats and I think they should have more weekday opportunities for fun and dancing. So in that way it is an entirely accurate predictor of my personality and world view.”_ And we’ve hit the last song in the countdown… ##1. [Richard](https://www.unboxedconsulting.com/people/richard-stobart): ## Jane Birkin et Serge Gainsbourg - Je T'aime,…Moi Non Plus

Richard’s comment: “Classy.”

What’s your anniversary song? Tweet us the video link with the #UnboxedIs10 hashtag and join us in sharing.
